About LP Security

Our Mission

To provide innovative, reliable, and professional security solutions that protect our clients' assets, people, and reputation. We are committed to excellence through continuous training, technology integration, and unwavering integrity.

Our Vision

To be East Africa's most trusted security partner, setting the standard for excellence in integrated security services while fostering a culture of loyalty, professionalism, and community safety.

Founded in 2012, under the DAMEVA Entreprises Ltd, Lakers Pride Security is one of the fastest growing security companies in Kenya.

Over the last decade,it has expanded geographically from its base in Nyanza and western Kenya to cover 15+ counties across the country, We have built a reputation for integrity, reliability, and excellence in security services.

Our approach combines highly trained personnel, modern technology, and a deep commitment to integrity. We don't just protect assets ? we build long-term partnerships based on trust and reliability.

With over 150 certified officers, 24/7 monitoring centers, and a rapid response team, we ensure your safety is never compromised. Our clients include hotels, Large Agricultural and industrialsectors, corporate offices, residential estates, and critical infrastructure.
